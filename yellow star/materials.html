Materials for Crochet Star Keychain

Back to Crochet Star Keychain

Materials (crochet part):

- Any yarn 4mm or thicker but I will be using 4mm worsted yellow yarn

- 5mm crochet hook (the bigger your hook, the more loose your top will be)

- Scissors

- Stitching needle

- Stuffing

- Stitch marker (you can use whatever you want for a stitch marker, it will only be used once)
