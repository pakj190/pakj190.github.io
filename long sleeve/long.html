This is my long sleeve mesh top

materials:

- any yarn of your preference but I will be using a 2mm cotton yarn (it's thinner than regular cotton yarn)

- 5mm crochet hook (the bigger your hook, the more loose your top will be)

- scissors

- tape measure or form of ruler (optional and only used for sizing)

- stitch marker (you can use whatever you want for a stitch marker, it will only be used once)

sizing:

you can make this any size you want so i will tell you how to extend the pattern to fit whatever size you desire.

I will be working with my own personal size that fits me best
