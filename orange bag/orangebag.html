we are making this mini mesh bag together

materials:

- 4mm worsted yarn (mine is orange)

- 5mm crochet hook (the bigger your hook, the more loose your top will be)

- scissors

- stitching needle

- stitch marker (you can use whatever you want for a stitch marker, it will only be used once)

this is my pattern that i made from scratch so finding a youtube tutorial might not exist
